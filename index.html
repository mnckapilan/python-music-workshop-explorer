Welcome to the Music Data Explorer 🎵

This tool lets you browse real songs stored as Python dictionaries — the same data your exercises use. Find a song you like, then see exactly how to access it in your Python code.

🔍
Search or filter

Type a song title, artist name, or album in the search bar. Use the genre dropdown to narrow things down.

🃏
Click any card

Find a song you recognise and click its card to open the Python code panel.

🐍
Copy the Python code

The panel shows you the exact lines to paste into your exercise — including the index number and every field value.

A note on the library: this is a sample of songs from 70 popular artists — roughly 9,000 tracks in total. It's not exhaustive, and your favourite song might not be here. That's totally fine! The data is just here to make the exercises feel more real.
